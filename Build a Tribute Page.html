Chinese Taipei baseball team

This team is the national team representing baseball of the Republic of China

Here's the team's history:

  • 1951 - For the first time, Taiwan has assembled domestic baseball elites in the form of a "star team" to travel to the Philippines for a friendly match. This is the first time to go abroad to compete.
  • 1954 - The Rizal Baseball Stadium in Manila, the capital of the Philippines, held the 1st Asian Championships, which was the first time for the ROC baseball team to participate in an international competition. There are only 4 teams participating in this tournament, which are the Republic of China, Japan, South Korea, and the Philippines, and these 4 teams are also founding members of the Asian Baseball Association, which was also established in May of that year. However, the first international match of the Republic of China ended in the bottom of all three matches.
  • 1955 - In the 2nd Asian Championships, the Republic of China won the second place with 3 wins and 3 losses.
  • 1983 Intercontinental Cup - In the Intercontinental Cup, the Chinese team beat the Cuban team 13:1, writing the record of beating Cuba, the world's baseball power, for the first time.
  • 1984 Los Angeles Olympics - In the Los Angeles Olympics, baseball became a demonstration project for the first time, and the Chinese team won the bronze medal.
  • 1984 World Cup - In the World Cup, the Chinese team defeated the US team 6:1, setting a record for the Chinese team to defeat the US team in an official international competition for the first time.
  • 1992 Barcelona Olympics - The baseball project became an official project for the first time, and the Chinese team won the silver medal. (This is Taiwan's best result in Olympic baseball)
  • 2001 World Cup Baseball - This series of events was held in Taiwan for the first time, with a total of 16 teams participating, making it the largest international competition in the history of Taiwanese baseball at that time. Among them, there were 25,000 spectators poured into the scene for the Chinese team and the American team, which was the record for the largest number of people entering a single game in Taiwan baseball at that time. This World Cup has the symbolic meaning of the Asian championship because it defeated Japan and South Korea in succession. This is also the third time Taiwan has won the third place in the history of the World Cup. It is worth mentioning that due to the success of this event, fans were attracted back to the stadium to support Taiwan's domestic professional baseball events.
  • 2006 Doha Asian Games - With a 5-game victory, they defeated South Korea, Thailand, China, the Philippines and Japan, and finally won the gold medal. This is the first time the Chinese team has won the gold medal since baseball was listed as an official event in the Asian Games.
  • 2007 World Cup Baseball - This series of events was held in Taiwan again, with a total of 16 teams participating. The Chinese team lost to Mexico 5:9 in the 5th preliminaries, 7:10 to the United States in the 7th preliminaries, and successively lost to the Netherlands, South Korea, Mexico, only got the 8th place in the session.
  • 2008 Beijing Olympics - The Chinese team defeated China for the first time by a score of 7:8 in a playoff (12 innings) that adopted the stalemate system.
  • 2009 World Baseball Classic - The Chinese team was eliminated with a 2-game losing streak. Not only was it the first country to be eliminated in this tournament, but it also lost to China for the second time in the official international competition with 1:4.
  • 2012 Asian Championships - In the battle between the Chinese team and the Korean team, they defeated the Korean team 7-0, which is the biggest lead the Chinese team has led the Korean team in a long time.
  • 2013 World Baseball Classic - Due to the poor record of the last Chinese team, this year must start from the qualifying round. The Chinese team entered the tournament with a scoreless victory in the qualifying round. The first two games in Group B of the preliminaries of the in-game preliminaries successfully defeated Australia and the Netherlands. Although they lost to South Korea by 2:3 in the last game, thanks to the Dutch victory over South Korea, they still entered the top 8 semi-finals with the first place in the group. . In the first round of the semi-finals, they fought with Japan in the 10th round and lost 3:4 by one point. In the second round, Cuba ended the game 14:0 ahead of schedule in the 7th round, becoming the first country to be eliminated in the semi-finals of this competition and missed the semi-finals. finals.
  • 2013 Tianjin East Asian Games preliminaries - Although the Chinese team ended Japan early (7 innings) with 11:0, they defeated China for the third time in the playoff (10 innings) by 3:4.
  • 2014 21U World Cup Baseball - The Chinese team sealed Japan 9-0 in the championship game, which is the first time in the history of a baseball team that it has won the championship in an official world competition.
  • 2015 The First World Baseball Top 12 Tournament - The Chinese team tied for the gold medal with Japan (the gold medal match was cancelled due to rain), and it was the first time in the history of a baseball team that it won the gold medal in a world multi-sport competition.
  • 2015 The First World Baseball Top 12 Tournament - After 29 years, the Chinese team once again defeated Cuba in the official international competition. (since the 1986 World Cup) but was still eliminated in the preliminaries and finished 9th.
  • 2016 U-23 World Cup Baseball - The Chinese team ranked 7th after being eliminated in the preliminaries.
  • 2017 World Baseball Classic - The Chinese team lost against Israel, the Netherlands, and South Korea (0 wins and 3 losses), so they were eliminated in the last place of the group, but they qualified for the next World Baseball Classic.
  • 2017 Summer Universiade Baseball Games - The Chinese team lost to France 3-4 in the preliminaries, defeated the Czech Republic 11-0, lost 3-6 to South Korea and lost two key games and were unable to advance to the semi-finals. Finished in 5th place after 0 wins over Mexico and Russia.
  • 2017 Asian Professional Baseball Championship - The Chinese team lost 0-1 to South Korea and 2-8 to Japan in the preliminaries, and finished third in the first competition. Wang Borong and Zhu Yuxian won the best ten players in the conference.
  • 2018 Jakarta Asian Games - In the first match of the preliminaries, it won 2-1 against South Korea. It was also the second time after 12 years after the 2006 Doha Asian Games that they defeated the South Korean team composed of professional baseball players in the international competition. It is worthy of praise. This time the Chinese team is composed of amateur players, so it is even more valuable. This Asian Games finally won the bronze medal.
  • 2019 Final of the Asian Baseball Championship - The Chinese team defeated the Japanese team 5:4. It was also the 18th year after the Asian Championships in 2001 that they defeated the Japanese team in the Asian Championships again and won the fifth championship in team history.
  • 2019 World Baseball Top 12 Rematch - The Chinese team played against the South Korean team at Chiba Ocean Stadium in Japan. Taiwan defeated South Korea 7-0, setting the largest win gap between Taiwan and South Korea in the first-level competition, and also leaving South Korea without a point. The final result of the World Top 12 Baseball Tournament was 2 wins and 3 losses, and the Chinese team finished fifth, which is also the best record of the Chinese team in international first-level competitions in the past 10 years.
